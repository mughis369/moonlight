FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I sign up for a Club Membership?

You can sign up in person, or through our online portal

When Will I be billed for my Wash Membership?

The day you sign up for your Wash Membership, will be your first day in the club. We will auto-draft your credit card on that same date each month as long as you are a member.

What happens if I get a new license plate or a new car?

Easy! Just come into the office and one of our customer service representatives will transfer your plan to reflect your new plate or vehicle.

Can I use my Wash Membership on more than one vehicle?

Wash Membership is specific to one vehicle. 

How do I cancel my Wash Membership?

We would hate to lose you, but if you need to cancel, simply come into the office and one of our manager on duty will help you cancel your plan. 

What happens when I cancel my Wash Membership?

Your plan will run out on the last day of your billing cycle. You will be billed for the full month.
